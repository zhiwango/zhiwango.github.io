About me

I am Chinese, came to Japan as an international student in 2014, now live in Tokyo.
I am a system engineer at TIER IV, before that, I graduated from the Saitama Institute of Technology. My interests are digital image processing and autonomous car, and I received a Ph.D in 2020.
